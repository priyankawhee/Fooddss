About TasteTrends Explorer

TasteTrends Explorer is an interactive platform that leverages food reviews and recipe data to provide valuable insights into food trends, reviewer contributions, and popular recipe categories. This project is built to explore sentiment analysis and data visualization for food enthusiasts and researchers.

Datasets Used

  • Recipes Dataset: Information on thousands of recipes, including ingredients, preparation instructions, and categories.
  • Reviews Dataset: User reviews and ratings for various recipes.
  • Cleaned and Merged Dataset: A combination of the above datasets for comprehensive analysis.

Analysis Goals

The primary objective of this project is to analyze food-related data and provide insights such as:

  • Identifying the most loved and least loved cuisines.
  • Spotting top authors based on contributions and consistency.
  • Understanding trends over time in the food domain.

Methodology

  • Data Cleaning: Removing duplicates, handling missing values, and merging datasets.
  • Sentiment Analysis: Detecting sentiments from user reviews.
  • Visualization: Displaying interactive insights with dashboards.

Future Enhancements

Possible improvements to the project include:

  • Real-time data updates for dynamic insights.
  • Advanced sentiment analysis for textual reviews.
  • User-uploaded recipe reviews and feedback.
